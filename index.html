About Us

Warped Logic was founded by Glenn McLaughlin and Garrett Webb from Calgary, Canada. One night over beers, Glenn and Garrett were sharing their ideas they each had for a board game and a computer game. After combining their two ideas together, they were left with what would later be called Pandora’s Box and another game yet to be named. After making a rough set of their first game, they were immediately hooked on the game making process.

Two years after the first conversation was had, in 2019 they will be launching their first game "Pandora’s Box" on kickstarter! Check back in the frequently or subscribe to their newsletter to be notified when you can get involved!
